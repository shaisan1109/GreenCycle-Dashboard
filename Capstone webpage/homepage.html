How can we think we can help you as Greencycle Innovative Solutions?

Since 2018, Greencycle has developed a track record of recovery/treatment/recycling Post Consumer Plastic Wastes for local and multinational companies that want to do their share in reducing plastic pollution consistent with the Circular Economy Model.

We have established multiple collection points and cooperation mechanism to all stakeholders involved in the value chain, (1) Junk Shops, (2) Local Government Units, (3) Sanitary Landfills, (4) Retailers, (5) Informal Waste Picker, (6) and (7) Recyclers, (8) Industrial Companies and (9) Plastic Waste Re-Users
